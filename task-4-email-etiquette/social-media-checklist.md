# Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist for College Students

## Do's
1. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
2. Think twice before posting content you wouldn't want an employer to see.
3. Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas.
4. Use professional language when engaging in academic or industry groups.
5. Report any instances of cyberbullying to the appropriate channels.
   
## Don'ts
1. Never share your passwords or OTPs online.
2. Avoid engaging with ""trolls"" or spreading unverified rumors.
3. Don't post sensitive personal info like your home address or ID.
4. Never post derogatory comments about your institution or peers.
5. Don't click on suspicious links or ""clickbait"" from unknown sources.
