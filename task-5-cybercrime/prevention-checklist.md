# Stay Safe Online' Checklist

1) Verify UPI Identity: Before making any payment, double-check the recipient's name in the app. Scammers often use names similar to official organizations.

2) No PIN for Receiving: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required for sending or checking your balance.

3) Audit Your Followers: Regularly check your follower list and remove any profiles that look fake (no posts, suspicious usernames, or no mutual friends).

4) Set Profiles to Private: Limit your social media visibility so that only "Friends" can see your posts and personal details.

5) Document Everything: If you are being harassed, do not delete the messages. Take screenshots as evidence for reporting.

6) Don't Engage: Responding to "trolls" or fake profiles often encourages the behavior. Block and move on immediately.

7) Limit Location Sharing: Avoid "checking in" to locations in real-time; post about your visits after you have left the venue.

8) Report to Authorities: Use the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930 for immediate assistance with any online fraud or harassment
